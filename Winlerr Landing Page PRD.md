# WINLERR LANDING PAGE PRD

## Product Requirements Document — Version 1.0

## 1. Product Name

**Winlerr**

## 2. Tagline

**Automate. Optimize. Grow.**

## 3. Product Summary

Winlerr is an invite-first business growth platform designed to help businesses improve their online presence, attract more customers, automate client engagement, plan better content, and manage digital workflows from one central system.

The first version of the website will be a high-converting landing page that explains the vision, collects waitlist signups, allows businesses to request a free website/prototype, and lets visitors suggest features they would want in the platform.

This landing page is not the full CRM yet. It is the public-facing early access page used for outreach, cold calls, social media marketing, and client validation.

## 4. Main Goal

The goal of the landing page is to convert business owners into early waitlist users by clearly explaining what Winlerr will help them do.

The page must make visitors feel:

* “This can help my business look more professional online.”
* “This can help me get more customers.”
* “This can save me time with marketing and client communication.”
* “I should join early and request a free prototype.”

## 5. Target Audience

Winlerr is mainly targeting small and growing businesses in Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, South Africa, and eventually global markets.

Primary users include:

* Local shops
* Restaurants
* Beauty salons
* Real estate agencies
* Schools and training centres
* Online sellers
* Service providers
* Agencies
* Clinics
* Small companies without a proper website
* Businesses struggling with social media consistency
* Businesses that receive messages through WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook

## 6. Core Landing Page Message

Most businesses are online, but not organized.

They post sometimes, reply late, struggle with content ideas, do not have proper websites, and miss potential customers because their digital presence is not connected.

Winlerr helps businesses bring everything together:

* Website presence
* Social media planning
* AI content ideas
* Customer communication
* WhatsApp/Instagram/Facebook automation
* Business insights
* Growth workflows
* Prototype requests
* Marketing support

### Section 2: Problem Section

Headline:

**Most Businesses Are Losing Customers Without Realizing It**

Copy:

Your customers are already searching, scrolling, comparing, and messaging online. But if your business has no website, inconsistent content, slow replies, or no clear digital system, people move on to competitors who look more prepared.

Winlerr is being built to solve this gap.

Problem cards:

1. **No Website Presence**
   Many businesses rely only on social media and miss customers who want a professional website.

2. **Inconsistent Marketing**
   Posting only when you remember makes growth slow and unpredictable.

3. **Slow Customer Replies**
   Late replies on WhatsApp, Instagram, or Facebook can cost real sales.

4. **No Content Direction**
   Many businesses do not know what to post, when to post, or how to say it.

5. **No Central System**
   Websites, messages, posts, designs, and customer interactions are scattered everywhere.

### Section 3: Solution Section

Headline:

**Winlerr Connects Your Online Business Workflow**

Copy:

Winlerr is designed to become your business growth control centre. From website setup to AI content planning, client engagement, social media workflows, and automation, the platform helps your business move with clarity and consistency.

Feature cards:

1. **Website Prototype Requests**
   Businesses can request a free prototype website based on their industry, brand style, and goals.

2. **AI Content Planning**
   Get content ideas, captions, campaign suggestions, and marketing direction based on your business niche.

3. **Social Media Workflow Planning**
   Plan posts, campaigns, reels, carousels, offers, and updates from one place.

4. **Client Engagement Automation**
   Prepare automated responses and customer follow-up flows for platforms like WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook.

5. **Business Growth Insights**
   Track what your business needs, what customers ask for, and how your online presence can improve.

6. **Design & Branding Support**
   Use tools like Canva-style workflows and ready-made templates to improve your brand visuals.
